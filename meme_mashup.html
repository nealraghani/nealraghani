"Mad at Gas Car"

A play-on-words toward the popular Dreamworks movie "Madagascar."

Medium(s): Adobe Photoshop


Meme Mashup: This meme is inspired by the popular Drake meme from his Cell Phone music video. Using the popular movie "Madagascar," I used the idea of distorting the name to have a completely different meaning, which resulted in using Elon Musk's innovation of the electric car as the other main subject of the meme. Baby Alex, on the left, is seen preferring a potential movie about electric cars as opposed to "Madagascar."
