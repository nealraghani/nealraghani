Glitch Images: The following images are from the anime series "Bleach". In the anime, the main character, Ichigo, undergoes "hollowfication" as he is granted his powers. Hollowfication distorts his appearance and sanity, almost behaving as though he's a rabid animal. Although Ichigo is portrayed as a caring and supportive character, he can lose himself when the situation is dire. Later in the series, the audience is introduced to Sosuke Aizen, a calm and collected man and one of the captains in the Gotei 13. After faking his own death and revealing his true intentions, he is now introduced as the main villain, stopping at nothing to become a god. After Aizen's betrayal, the audience is introduced to Ulquirra Cifer, one of Aizen's "Espada." Each of the Espada represents an aspect of death, with Ulquiorra representing nihilism, often portraying himself as lifeless and depressed. When he is ultimately beaten by Ichigo, he fades away, finally expressing how Ichigo made him understand what it's like to be a human.
