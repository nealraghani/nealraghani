"POV: Your Friend Gets You Out of Jail"

A skit on a prison inmate being set free by his friend just so that he could show the inmate his new hat.

Medium(s): FiLMiC Pro, Adobe Premiere Pro


Editing: Our next project, and the project that would kickstart the usage of Adobe, was editing. The class was to create a short skit that incorporates basic editing in Premiere Pro. Being the last introductory project before intensive editing was required, the project was also a means of testing our basic storytelling and editing skills. For my skit, I chose to create a short comedy on a prison inmate being set free by his friend to show the inamte his new hat. Confused, he then wishes he was back in the cell.
