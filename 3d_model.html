3D Modeling: Sculpting has existed as an influential part of human history. For example, the Benin Bronzes, sculpted by Nigerians many centuries ago, serve as monumental pieces of African culture and how the people have survived and emerged. Sculpting can be expressive in retelling the history of events or people. In some cases, sculpting can have religious purposes, showing appreciation and loyalty to a respective God. However, sculpting for the purpose of telling a story is what I have found most interesting. With the integration of 3D modeling, one can implement sculpting elements that would be otherwise difficult in regular sculpting. Lighting, environments, and natural effects, like burning, are but a few of the many ways 3D modeling can amplify storytelling and sculpting altogether.
