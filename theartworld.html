The Art World
PowerPoint

The Art World PowerPoint series was commissioned by San José State University professor and contemporary art curator Lance Fung for a lecture series at Santa Clara University. This three-part presentation not only traces Fung’s professional journey as a curator, but also offers an in-depth look into the inner workings of the contemporary art world. Topics include major contemporary galleries, international art events, key industry figures, art in the public realm, and the role of public engagement in shaping public art. The series was met with positive critical feedback, with particular praise for its clarity, accessibility, and effective use of animation and structured design.
