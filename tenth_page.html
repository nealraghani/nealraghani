Neal Raghani is a digital media artist who specializes in graphic designing and design planning. He currently lives in San Ramon, California. Having been interested in graphic design since high school, Neal has developed various skills through his craft.

Having been inspired by many forms of media throughout the years, Neal has a strong suit in tackling various subjects, albeit broad and controversial subjects in today's continuum. By using various resources in the realm of digital media art and graphic design, which communicates to a broad audience, he wants to create a safe and welcoming space for healing and ambience.
