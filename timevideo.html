"When You Log Onto Your Zoom Class"

A video detailing my perspective of logging onto Zoom either too early or when there is no class.

Medium(s): FiLMiC Pro, Adobe Premiere Pro


Perception of Time: To become familiar with Art 75, the class to film a short video based on the perception of time. Because this project served to introduce the class to basic video elements, intensive editing was not required. What did matter, however, was the way in which the artist made use of video transitions to ensure time and the story was articulated and consistent. For my project, I wanted to create a video based on my experiences of logging onto Zoom either when class is not scheduled or when I am too early.
