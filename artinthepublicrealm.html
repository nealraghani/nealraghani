ART IN THE PUBLIC REALM

Fung Collaboratives, Santa Clara University

Commissioned by contemporary art curator Lance Fung for his 3-part lecture series at Santa Clara University about how art in the public realm positively transforms people and locations. The first in his lecture series, this presentation illustrates the broader definition of art and the qualities of an impactful artwork in a public space.
