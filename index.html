A Message from the Artist: This website and the subsections above are dedicated to the compilations of projects that have tested my creative mindset and, therefore, demonstrate my dexterity over a number of the limitless mediums of art that exist today. I believe an artist's portfolio is more than just a phone number you give to a client, or something to brag about to your friends. Portfolios serve to embody the soul of the artist, propagating how the world could be theirs with just a thought of the mind. Liken the artist's portfolio to a samurai's katana: both demostrate calculated, attentive, and careful strokes. Each day that both the sword and the portfolio awaken to is a chance to inspire and lead the inner creative geniuses of the world. I hope my work illuminates such a message to you: the viewer. If my work inspires you and captures your interests, visit the "About Me" page to learn more and to contact.
