ARTS KURA (REALIZED VISION)

Fung Collaboratives, Kura Kura Bali, Museum of Modern Art

Developed for Fung Collaboratives and Kura Kura Bali ahead of the Museum of Modern Art’s (MoMA) 2026 site visit, this presentation outlines the context and history behind the joint conception of the 'Constellations' series and ARTS Kura. It details the master plans for an international architecture competition, an artist residency, and a future museum, articulating Fung and Kura Kura Bali’s shared mission to bring this ambitious cultural vision to life.
