Neal Raghani is a Bay Area artist whose work explores various aspects of his identity throughout his life. Working in primarily new media, Raghani’s art raises questions about strength and courage when overcoming hardships, and how perseverance reshapes one’s character. Empathizing with the viewer is a fundamental aspect of his work, as the artist connects his past challenges with the present to examine different ways of communing with people.

Digital illustration features prominently in his practice, as this medium acts to stitch moments of his life together fluidly such that he transforms his identity into a story. By sharing his story, he relates to others, as connections between people’s lives and emotions are made visible. His work also references objects, people, and locations, highlighting how they function as key devices in overcoming his difficulties and, thus, becoming a new person.
