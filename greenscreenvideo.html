"When You Listen to Lil Uzi Vert Too Much"

A skit detailing how I feel when I listen to Lil Uzi Vert when I go out in public.

Medium(s): Adobe Premiere Pro


Green Screen & Motion Tracking: The second to last project for Art 75 was centered around making use of green screen and motion tracking. Green screen and motion tracking are implemented in many digital video artworks, and learning how to use both effectively was the goal for this assignment. For my project, I based my inspiration off something that has become an integral part of my life: music. When I listen to music when I work, I try to visualize an image or video in correlation to the emotion the song is in tune with. This is especially the case when I listen to Lil Uzi Vert.
