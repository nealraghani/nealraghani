Fung Collaboratives
Business Cards

Commissioned by contemporary art curator Lance Fung, I designed business cards for his company, Fung Collaboratives, as well as for project director John Talley. Featuring the company’s logo and mascot, Buster, the designs were intended to convey themes of prosperity and good fortune, symbolized by Buster’s rightward gaze.
