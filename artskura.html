ARTS Kura
Wix.com

ARTS Kura is a proposed arts organization to be located in Bali, envisioned as the largest of its kind in Southeast Asia. The institution would host annual international architecture competitions, a contemporary art museum, and an artist residency program. Guided by the architectural vision of Lance Fung and Kura Kura Bali, ARTS Kura aims to be among the first arts organizations to significantly strengthen and advance contemporary art in the region. This vision, presented as a mock website, was pitched to Kura Kura Bali and renowned businesswoman and philanthropist Cherie Nursalim. The proposal was enthusiastically received, with both Kura Kura Bali and Nursalim praising the website for its elegant and effective communication of a complex, multifaceted concept.
