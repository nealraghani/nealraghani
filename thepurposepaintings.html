"THE PURPOSE PAINTINGS"

2022, screen prints on canvas

The Purpose Paintings demonstrate the haunting reality of people living their lives guided by their purpose. Purpose, which can be described as one’s central motivating aims of their life, can easily influence their behavior, decisions, perceptions, and work. As such, while people can act with the purest intentions at heart under this influence, selfish and harmful decisions will inevitably occur. These decisions can severely affect oneself or the lives of many, perhaps leading to their death. In spite of everything pure people do for the good of the world, impulses of greed will soon control them and the public will begin to ridicule.

As stated previously, any position of influence possesses the inevitability of ensuing pain onto others. In the contemporary world, regardless if one is a CEO of a corporation or a street performer, they will make selfish and inhumane decisions. Whether conscious or not, these decisions are enacted with differing intentions in mind, but the most common intent is to achieve power. This corrupt game of power disillusions the good in respected positions and the morality of those whose purposes are attributed to those positions. The Purpose Paintings asks the audience whether their actions are justifiable and if they should be forgiven.

The topic of purpose is important to The Purpose Paintings because of what it means for future generations. Behind the selfishness of people, there also lies a desire to aid humanity into a better future. It is important to realize self-centered tendencies from different power positions in order to achieve the greatest good for the most people. By exposing this behavior in human beings, The Purpose Paintings advocates future generations to set a better example of pursuing purpose and to lead the world with compassion rather than greed.
