"A Mouse from the Future"

A mouse from the future, now a robot, details his past life and how he was happy during that time.

Medium(s): Adobe Audition, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro


Sound: The next project after editing was based on sound. Sound is an important element in digital video, and it's paramount to ensure the sound in a video is leveled in such a way that it does not leave the audience discontent with the project. For my project, my goal was to make use of my voice and ensure the quality of it was balanced and matched the narrative of the video. I also wanted uniqueness, so I edited my voice in Adobe Audition to be that of a robot to match the story of my skit.
