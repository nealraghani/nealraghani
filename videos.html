Digital Videos: The world of digital video is growing rapidly. YouTube videos, blockbuster movies, and short films have introduced a unique form of art that is only becoming more versatile. Unlike digital art and drawing, digital videos are composed of frames, which are treated like paintings or a digital art piece. Additionally, edits are made to enhance the lighting and visuals for a diverse setting and storytelling. Greenscreen and motion-tracking are innovative technologies that continuously push digital video to new heights and possibilities. With these technologies and mediums, digital video has created messages and stories that have popularized modern art and culture, inspiring other creative artists looking to make an impact.
