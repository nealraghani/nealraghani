"SUPERNOVA: SWEET LIKE CANDY"

2026, digital video

"Supernova: Sweet Like Candy" is an audio-reactive piece that explores the emotion of ecstacy as a three-dimensional, kinetic object. Unlike water, a substance that takes on the shape of it's receptacle, overwhelming emotions of joy, rapture, and bliss break through their vessel, becoming uncontained like an explosion.
