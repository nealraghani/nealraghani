AI Artwork: Artificial Intelligence and art are a group slowly making their way into mainstream art culture. Though in it's infancy, the group is producing shocking results in a short amount of time. In Art 74, the class was asked to test Runway, an AI art website, an draw an environment. Using different textures as brushes, the class was able to produce environments that appeared almost photo-realistic. Next, the class was asked to take photographs or paintings and render them in Runway under a different one. In my case, I borrowed three Ukiyo-e paintings and rendered them in an oil color appearance.
