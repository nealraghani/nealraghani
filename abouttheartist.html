About the Artist: Neal Raghani is a digital artist who lives in San Ramon, California. He has acquired a strong passion for digital media at a young age and, since then, has developed various skills from different mediums. Graphic design, 3D modeling, drawing, painting, and videography the skills he has honed over his time as a digital artist.

Currently, he is studying digital media art at San Jose State University and is looking to get his BFA.

From posters, album covers, paintings to 3D models his artwork tackles a wide variety of subjects. His attention to detail and usage of symbolism brings his works to life.
