"The Urban Legend of the Butterfly Man"

A longer skit about a researcher in the 1900s who visited a tribe possessing wisdom on the advancement and harmony of human life.

Medium(s): Adobe Audition, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro


Final: For the Art 75 final, we were to create a 5-6 minute video based on the editing techniques we have learned. We were then documented on the process of creating the project and were given feedback each week. We could either emphasize on one particular skill or use the vast array that we learned together in the final. For my project, I was inspired by the many Asian philosophical works, particularly the ones by Lao Tzu and Buddha. The goal behind the video was to demonstrate to the audience that today's society focuses on the past and future more so than the present, and prioritizes smaller matter a great deal than matters that could impact the longevity of human existence.
