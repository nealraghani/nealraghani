"House on the Hill"

Grassy hilled environment with a house overlooking the sunset.

Medium(s): Blender


Blender Environment: Our next assignment after the remodel was to create a 3D environment. Our instructor demonstrated to the class essential environmental elements that we could use for our projects. Hills, mountains, bodies of water, and rocky grounds were among the many features we could add to our environment. In mine, I chose to create an environment with hills and grass, as I wanted to create a similar setting to my home. The house was added to simulate the setting better.
