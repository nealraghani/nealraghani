ZBrush Heads: ZBrush is a 3D sculpting application and is one of the most frequently used sculpting applications. Though a subscription is available in ZBrush, the free version allows for creativity in sculpting character models and heads. Environments can also be created, though takes a great amount of patience and dexterity. In this project, Art 74 was required to experiment with ZBrush to create two human heads and one alien head. With my alien head, I decided to sculpt the helmet of a character from the popular iOS RPG Infinity Blade.
