"Slow Burn"

Statue downloaded from Blend Swap and reanimated with a surrounding environment while burning away.

Medium(s): Blender, Blend Swap


Remodel of Blender Model: After ZBrush, Art 74 was required to explore Blender, another commonly used 3D sculpting application. After testing the water with the program by creating a donut, the class was navigated to a community forum called Blend Swap, dedicated to sharing Blender creations to the world for people to download. After selecting our chosen asset, the class was then directed to distort, add to, or remodel it to change it's meaning and the message behind it. I chose a statue and added an environment and a burning effect to the statue to present the message that lust and infatuation are all things in love that rarely survive.
